How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account and associated data from Zina Erbil, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Zina Erbil app and log in to your account.
  2. From the home screen, tap the bottom menu and select Profile.
  3. from Profile screen, Tap the edit icon in the top-right corner.
  4. In your Profile screen, tap Delete Your Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.
